LM335A for Arduino

There's been many different temperature sensors over the years but the LM335A is a classic. It runs on 2.7 - 5.9V and outputs an analog signal of 0.1V per degree. This makes it very easy to use with any microprocessor with an ADC or Analog to Digital Convertor.
